# Project PRD: ApplyFlow — AI Career Operating System ## 1. Executive Summary ApplyFlow is a premium, AI-native career operating system designed for ambitious professionals. It bridges the gap between job discovery and landing an offer by automating the repetitive, stressful, and inefficient parts of the job application process. ## 2. Product Vision & Goals * **Vision:** To be the "intelligent copilot" for high-achieving professionals, providing a futuristic, emotionally supportive, and highly productive job-hunting experience. * **Goals:** * Reduce time-to-apply by 80% via AI automation. * Increase interview match rates through precision resume tailoring. * Centralize career momentum in a high-fidelity command center. * Provide real-time coaching for interviews and negotiations. ## 3. Target Audience * Elite professionals (Designers, Engineers, Product Managers) looking for their next high-impact role. * "Ambitious job hunters" who value speed, efficiency, and premium tools. ## 4. Key Features & User Stories ### A. Chrome Extension (The Signature Experience) * **LinkedIn AI Overlay:** Real-time match scoring, salary estimation, and recruiter insights injected directly into job boards. * **One-Click Auto-Fill:** AI-powered form detection and automated application submission. * **Instant Tailoring:** Generate job-specific cover letters and resumes without leaving the browser. ### B. AI Resume Lab * **ATS Optimization:** Neural mapping of resumes against job descriptions. * **Skills Gap Analysis:** Identification of missing keywords and impact-driven bullet point rewriting (Before vs. After). * **Master Resume Management:** Version control for specialized professional identities. ### C. Career Command Center (Dashboard) * **Application Pipeline:** Kanban-style tracking (Saved, Applied, Interviewing, Offer). * **AI Insights:** Proactive recommendations on career velocity and market demand. * **Interview Tracker:** Management of panels, preparation kits, and follow-up reminders. ### D. AI Interview Prep & Copilot * **Mock Interviews:** AI-led behavioral and technical practice sessions. * **Real-time Feedback:** Analysis of speech speed, filler words, and STAR-method adherence. * **Career Copilot Chat:** 24/7 access to negotiation strategies, job fit analysis, and career roadmap advice. ## 5. Design & Aesthetic Principles * **Theme:** "Lumina" — Premium Dark Mode. * **Colors:** Deep charcoal (#131313) backgrounds, soft electric blue highlights, violet AI glow accents. * **Inspirations:** Linear (minimalism), Arc Browser (translucency), Vercel (clean typography), Stripe (organized complexity). * **Vibe:** Calm, intelligent, fast, and automation-first. * **Typography:** Geist/Inter family for technical precision. ## 6. Technical Requirements (AI-Native) * **LLM Integration:** Fine-tuned models for professional narrative crafting. * **Neural Mapping:** Vector-based matching of candidate history to job requirements. * **Automation Engine:** Intelligent DOM manipulation for browser-based autofill. ## 7. Success Metrics * **Match Rate:** Percentage of applications resulting in interviews. * **Time Saved:** Reduction in manual data entry and tailoring time. * **Confidence Score:** User-reported sentiment of preparedness and career momentum.
